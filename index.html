Anik Pramanik

Machine Learning Researcher · Senior Data Analyst, ADN DigiNet Ltd. · Dhaka, Bangladesh
Email GitHub Google Scholar

I work on applied machine learning for healthcare. My research uses deep learning to detect disease from clinical images, predict clinical risk from patient data, and forecast epidemiological trends — alongside work on natural language processing for Bengali, a low-resource language. I have 11 peer-reviewed publications across these areas.

Research Interests

Medical Image Analysis
Deep learning and transfer learning for disease detection from clinical images — monkeypox lesion classification and lung disease from chest X-rays.
Clinical Risk Prediction
Ensemble methods for predicting patient outcomes from heterogeneous clinical and behavioral data — chronic kidney disease, Parkinson's, and depression screening.
Epidemiological Forecasting
Time-series modelling of disease spread to support public health planning, including ARIMA and SARIMA forecasting of monkeypox transmission.
Low-Resource NLP
Language models for Bengali, where annotated corpora are scarce — sentiment analysis of social media and text classification with LSTM architectures.
